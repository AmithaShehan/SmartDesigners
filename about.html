Our Story

Back in 2016, university student Sathya Wimarshana had a vision. A Mechanical and Process Engineering Graduate with a thirst for creativity, he saw the potential for technology to tell stories and shape dreams. Thus, Smart Designers was born, a haven where imagination blossoms through innovative design and meticulous craftsmanship.

Our canvas is diverse, our passion singular.

We bring your ideas to life in vibrant dimensions, whether it's:

  • 3D Designs: From intricate prototypes to custom-made masterpieces, our experts breathe life into your vision with unparalleled precision.
  • Lithophane Lamps: Memories become magical as we transform photographs into captivating 3D illuminations, casting a warm glow on cherished moments.
  • 3D Printing: We don't just print objects, we print possibilities. Explore a world of limitless creation with our diverse range of services and expertise.
  • Woodworking: Nature's finest materials meet human ingenuity, resulting in unique pieces that showcase the beauty and warmth of wood.
  • Beyond the Ordinary: We don't shy away from a challenge. Whether it's crafting custom furniture or building cutting-edge 3D printing machines, our passion fuels our drive to innovate.

Smart Designers isn't just a business, it's a global community. From Sri Lanka to corners of the world, our creations adorn homes and spark imaginations. We believe in the power of design to connect hearts and minds, one personalized masterpiece at a time.

Ready to turn your vision into reality? Come explore the world of Smart Designers, where the only limit is your imagination.
